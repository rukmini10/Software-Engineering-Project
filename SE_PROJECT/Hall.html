Naming Ceremony

The Barasala or Naam Karan is the traditional ceremony where a newborn is lovingly given a name on an auspicious date. Usually, the entire house is cleaned and pujas are performed followed by the main ceremony. Because it is such an important or rather, landmark event in a child's life, it is celebrated with great pomp and show by many. Guests are invited, menu is selected, priest and venue are decided etc. If you're looking forward to hosting the naming ceremony of your little one, this is the best place.
